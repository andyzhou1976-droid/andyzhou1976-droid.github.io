About QingHong

Cangzhou Qinghong Machinery Manufacturing Co., Ltd. was established in 1998. Now it is a foundry enterprise with more than 20 years casting experience. Convenient transportation, close to Beijing, only one hour's distance from Tianjin. Since its establishment, the company has been focusing on the production of nodular graphite castings.

Our company mainly produces spheroidal graphite well cap, water grate and pipe fittings, covering an area of 33300 square meters. There are more than 90 employees.
