CangZhou QingHong Foundry Co., LTD.

Mar. 05, 2019
Share

Established in 1998, CANGZHOU QINGHONG FOUNDRY CO., LTD. is a foundry enterprise with more than 20 years experience in casting. Located in XINJIANBOLI VILLAGE, WANGWU TOWN, BOTOU CITY HEBEI CHINA, we are close to Beijing and Tianjin, espcially near to Tianjin with one hour's ride.

Since the establishment, we have been focusing on the production of nodular casting iron. We mainly produce ductile iron manhole cover, water grate and pipe fittings.

In 2017, we took a reformation, therefore we reached the standard of enterprise and the emission limit of green casting. We are a "green foundry enterprise", with an area of 33, 000 square meters, one vertical line and one horizontal line, and one dipping line. There are more than 90 employees, including 3 technicians, 3 laboratory personnel and 5 inspectors. The annual productivity reaches 12000 tons.

Our company now has two authoritative product certifications:

I. UK SGS UNITED LINGDOM LTD Certificate (obtained in 2011)

II. the latest version of EN124-2: 2015 product certificate of ICMQ S. P. A in Italy ( obtained in 2019/3/15)

Our laboratory now has spectrometers, tension machines, sand inspections, metallographic inspections, furnace front analyzers, temperature measurement and make quality control every day.

From product design, mould making, sample inspection, small batch trial production and mass production, raw materials and accessories into the factory, smelting, modeling, casting, casting cleaning, warehousing, and after-sales service, we have an adequate quality assurance system, and have passed the quality system certification in 2000 as well as the latest version of ISO 9001:2015 quality management system certification in 2017 to ensure we reach the requirements of customers for products. After years of efforts, we have won wide trust and praise in the region and industry. Our products are exported to more than 20 countries, including Italy, Spain, Portugal, Germany, Britain, Chile, Poland, Morocco, Serbia, Lebanon, Sri Lanka and so on.

We believe in "quality for survival" hence we will continue to adhere to our business philosophy of "integrity-based, service-oriented" .
